EU Safetygate report

Total Annual Alerts Issued

Over the last few years, the EU Safetygate member states issue safety alerts for on average around 2,000 - 2,200 safety alerts per year.

  • Around 25% of all alerts issued are for products where the Country of Origin is unknown.
  • On average China represents around 50% of Alerts issued

YoY Change for Alerts Issued

2023 saw a 59% increase in the average number of alerts issued in a year, with the total alerts issued increasing from 2,100 alerts in 2022 to 3,349 in 2023, an increase of 1,249 over the previous year.

2023 was an exception with the introduction of the ban on the chemical 'lilial / BMHCA' for use in cosmetics.

  • In 2023 1,087 safety alerts were issued for products containing BMHCA
  • Around 30% of these were issued to products manufactured in Italy

Economic Area of Product origin

This is an engineered feature from the data, this splits results by Economic Area which groups the countries under :

  • EU Countries within Schengen or European Economic Area (Ex Italy for visibility of 2023 alerts)
  • Italy - Italy represents around 30% of all alerts issued in 2023
  • Outside EU - Every other country not within the Eu Countries
  • China - because China represents such a large proportion of Global Trade
