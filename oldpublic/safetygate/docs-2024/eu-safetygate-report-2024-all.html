EU Safetygate report

YTD Annual Alerts Issued

Over the last few years, the EU Safetygate member states issue safety alerts for on average around 2,000 - 2,200 safety alerts per year.

  • Around 25% of all alerts issued are for products where the Country of Origin is unknown.
  • On average China represents around 50% of Alerts issued

This report covers the same time period for all years show, from start of year until the noted week number.

YoY Change for Alerts Issued

2024 (comparing up to the same Week number Year on Year) saw an 18% increase in the average number of alerts issued in a year, with the total alerts issued increasing from 748 alerts in 2023 to 943 so far in 2024, an increase of 145 over the same period, the previous year.

2022-2023 were exceptions, with the introduction of the ban on the chemical 'lilial / BMHCA' for use in cosmetics.

  • In 2022 156 safety alerts were issued for products containing BMHCA
  • In 2023 833 safety alerts were issued for products containing BMHCA
  • In 2024 165 safety alerts were issued for products containing BMHCA

So far in 2024 around 17% of all alerts issued so far this year have been for Cosmetics

Economic Area of Product origin

This is an engineered feature from the data, this splits results by Economic Area which groups the countries under :

  • EU Countries within Schengen or European Economic Area (Ex Italy for visibility of 2023 alerts)
  • Italy - Italy represents around 30% of all alerts issued in 2023
  • Outside EU - Every other country not within the Eu Countries
  • China - because China represents such a large proportion of Global Trade
  • Unknown Country of origin has been excluded
